about

us

Diagnosexpress is a diagnostic platform that provides online access to personalised medical services for all. We can offer fast assessment turnaround, expert diagnosis, and excellent reports from fully qualified scientists. Helping clinicians and patients start the health journey sooner, we focus on providing accessible and affordable services for everyone.

We work together with each client to define relationships that complement the overall best experience you get in healthcare, ensuring we can assist clients and medical professionals with customised healthcare solutions.

Our “Patients First” philosophy is at the heart of Diagnosexpress. The goal of our Patient Experience team is to create an environment that delivers world-class clinical care. In addition, we value face-to-face interaction as an important healing factor.

Our best practices comprise tools, techniques and methods that are measurable, replicable and evidence-based, all designed to enhance our clients’ experience. We actively engage our caregivers to develop and implement best practices throughout the client’s journey.

We are committed to following policies and standards of conduct to ensure Diagnosexpress is a safe place for both our clients and caregivers.
